About Me

I am a Videographer/Photographer that is based in San Diego, California. My goal is to create for a living. I love to make videos of things that I love. I go around and see and try to find new spots and places that haven't been found. Give a new perspective. I love taking photos of interesting spots, beautiful places, give them a message that requires thought when you look at them. That provoke something within you. I enjoy the creative process. I will never give up on my dream, for it will come true. The end, of a new beginning. - Gx0st
